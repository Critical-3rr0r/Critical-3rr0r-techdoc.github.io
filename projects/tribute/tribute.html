A timeline of Professor Stephen Hawking's life

  • 1942: Stephen Hawking is Born
  • 1958: Stephen Hawking leaves St.Albans School
  • 1959: Hawking Attends Oxford University
  • 1962: Hawking Receives First-Class Honors Degree
  • 1963: Hawking is Diagnosed with ALS
  • 1965: Hawking Writes his Thesis and Gets Married
  • 1966: Hawking Awarded Fellowship and Recieves Adams Prize
  • 1968: Hawking Becomes a Member of Institute of Astronomy
  • 1969: Hawking Health Deteriorates
  • 1970: Second Law of Black Hole Dynamics Discovered
  • 1973: Hawking Releases First Novel
  • 1975: Hawking Recieves the Pius XI Gold Medal for Science
  • 1977: Hawking Becomes a Professor
  • 1978: Hawking Recieves Honorary Doctorate from Oxford
  • 1979: Awarded Renowned Teaching Post at Cambridge
  • 1982: Awarded a CBE by the Queen of the United Kingdom
  • 1985: Stephen Loses his Voice on a Trip to Geneva
  • 1988: Hawking Releases 'A Brief History of Time'
  • 1992: Steven Spielberg Produces 'A Brief History of Time'
  • 1995: Hawking Gets Divorced and Marries Nurse
  • 2001: Hawking Publishes 'The Universe in a Nutshell'
  • 2004: Hawking Solved the Black Hole Paradox
  • 2006: Hawking and Elain(Nurse) Gets Divorced
  • 2009: Hawking Receives the US Medal of Freedom
  • 2014: The Release of 'The Theory of Everything'
  • 2018: Stephen Hawking Dies Peacefully
